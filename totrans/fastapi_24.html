Appendix B. Creatures and Humans

From ghoulies and ghosties
And long-leggetty beasties
And things that go bump in the night
Good Lord deliver us.

Verse from a Cornish litany

Imaginary creatures, or cryptids, have been reported everywhere. Some animals, once considered imaginary—like the panda, platypus, and black swan—turned out to be real. So we won’t speculate. Intrepid explorers are seeking them out. Together, they provide data for the examples in this book.
